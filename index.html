ONE OF DRIFTING'S MOST ICONIC FIGURES

Vaughn is known best by his aggressive, foot to the floor, big smoke driving style and is the face of the Ford Mustang RTR. In 2010, Vaughn earned his first Formula Drift Championship. Also in 2010, Vaughn launched his brand for a new generation of Mustang with the RTR. The dealer-installed package for the 2010-2015 Ford Mustang is available at select Ford dealerships worldwide.
